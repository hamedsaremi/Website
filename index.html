My name is Hamed Qahri-Saremi. My students usually call me "Dr. Hamed"...

 

I am an Assistant Professor of Information Systems at the College of Computing and Digital Media, DePaul University in Chicago, IL. I have a Ph.D. in business administration with specialization on information systems from the DeGroote School of Business, McMaster University, Hamilton, Ontario, Canada.

 

My research is focused on the design and use of social media technologies and their impacts on users, businesses, and the society. I investigate the reception and expression effects of electronic word of mouth on users and organizations. I also study the unintended and problematic patterns of use of online social platforms, such as unsafe information security behaviors, and their consequences for the users and organizations.

 

My teaching interests are in the areas of business analytics, applied data science, business process mining and management, enterprise systems, and business systems analysis and design. I am an SAP certified associate and have taught several SAP professional certificate courses to university students.
